LIFE MANUAL - THE BHAGAWAT GITA

Bhagavad Gita is the song of God. It is not only a historical epic, but a way of life. When we buy a new gadget, we cannot use it until reading its guide manual. Similarly, Bhagavad Gita is the guide manual of our life. HH Bhakti Ashraya Vaisnava Swami Maharaj emphasises reading, studying, contemplating and implementing Bhagavad Gita from the very beginning of our lives. It not only gives us direction to live life, but also unveils our true purpose on this planet.
